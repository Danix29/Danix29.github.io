Writing Exercise: Order the Paragraphs

Order the following paragraphs so that the text makes sense. Write the corresponding letter for each paragraph in the correct order (for example: H, D, G, A, B, E, F, C).

H: Dear (Mr/Ms) Green
D: I have seen your advertisement in this month's edition of English Today and would like to apply for a grant to improve my English in one of your schools this summer.
G: My main reason for applying is that I lack funds to both pay for tuition fees and stay in the UK for more than one month. A grant is the only way I can pay for these expenses as I am not allowed to work in the UK as an international student.
A: This is important as improving my level of spoken English would allow me to apply for a job as a teacher in my country, where teachers are required to have a C1 level.
B: Regarding the skills I would like to focus on, I am particularly interested in improving my speaking level. Although I practise with series in English, I feel my level would greatly improve if I could learn more about pronunciation in your course and practise after class with native speakers.
E: For these reasons I consider I am an ideal candidate for the grant, and thus hope you will consider my application favourably.
F: Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to hearing from you soon.
C: Your sincerely, Elsa Pato
