Complete the Letter with the Correct Phrase

Select the correct phrase (A-H) for each numbered gap (1-8) and reconstruct the formal letter. One phrase is extra and will not be used.

[1] I am writing to apply for the English Language Scholarship advertised in last week's edition of The Language Learner magazine.
[2] My current level of English (B2) allows me to communicate effectively in academic settings, but I particularly struggle with writing complex essays and understanding idiomatic expressions.
[3] For this reason, I believe participating in your intensive writing workshop and cultural immersion program would significantly enhance my ability to prepare proper research papers in my field of environmental engineering.
[4] This financial support is crucial as international students in Canada cannot work off-campus during their first year of study, making it impossible to cover both tuition and living expenses independently.
[5] I would specifically benefit from the daily academic writing clinics and the opportunity to collaborate with native speakers on sustainability projects, as shown in your program brochure.
[6] Having volunteered as a bilingual guide during the COP26 conference, I am committed to using improved English skills to facilitate international scientific collaborations.
[7] I would be grateful if you could consider my application for partial funding to attend the full 8-week program.
[8] Yours sincerely, Carlos Méndez

Select the correct phrase for each gap:

  • A) My main motivation for seeking this scholarship is limited financial resources.
  • B) Please find attached my academic transcripts and two recommendation letters.
  • C) The scholarship committee should select me because I'm very intelligent.
  • D) This practical experience aligns perfectly with your program's focus on real-world language applications.
  • E) Furthermore, I aim to develop stronger presentation skills through your public speaking seminars.
  • F) Thank you for considering my application. I remain available for any additional information required.
  • G) I enclose copies of my passport and vaccination certificate for your review.
  • H) Last summer, I participated in an exchange program where I first recognized these language gaps.
