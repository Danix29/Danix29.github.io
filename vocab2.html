Fill in the Gaps

1. The city council is building more affordable houses. - More affordable houses (Passive)

2. People say that immigration has improved cultural diversity. - Immigration (Impersonal passive)

3. We (have) our houses . (install) with solar panels to reduce energy consumption. (Causative)

4. In some countries, elections (monitor) by internal observers. (Passive)

5. It (say) that technology is increasing the gap between generations. (Impersonal)

6. The government (report) to be working on a new policy for climate change. (Impersonal)

7. He (get) his passport every ten years. (Causative)

8. Public transport (improve) in many cities to reduce pollution. (Passive)

9. It (believe) that urbanization will continue to grow in the next decade. (Impersonal)

10. We (expect) to receive new policies on mental health soon. (Personal)

11. Many human rights violations (document) by international organizations. (Passive)

12. She (have) her house (clean) by a professional service every week. (Causative)
